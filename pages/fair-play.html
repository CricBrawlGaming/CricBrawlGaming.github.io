Fair Play and Safety

  • 18+ Requirement: All users must be at least 18 years old to participate in our contests.
  • Single Account Policy: Each user is allowed only one account to maintain fairness.
  • Secure Verification: Our verification process ensures that deposits and withdrawals are secure and that each user uses payment methods linked to their verified identity.
  • Transparency in Contests: Users can download all teams in any live contest to verify fair play.
  • Employee Restrictions: Employees are prohibited from participating in real money contests on CricBrawl.
  • Responsible Gaming: CricBrawl promotes responsible gaming practices.
  • Entry Limits: Contests limit the maximum entries any single user can submit, giving everyone a fair shot at winning.
  • Prohibition of Bots: The use of bots or automated tools to enter contests or place bets is strictly forbidden.
  • Experience Badges: Each player's level is identified with an "Experience" badge, so users know the level of their competitors.
  • Anti-Collusion Policy: Collusion or teamwork between different users is strictly forbidden. Accounts found colluding will be permanently banned.
  • Reporting Violations: If you suspect any violation of fair play, such as collusion or the use of bots, please submit details of the occurrence or contact us immediately at info@cricbrawl.com so we can address the issue promptly.
